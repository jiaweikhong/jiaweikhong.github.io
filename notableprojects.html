Notable Projects

Asian Leadership Programme (ALP) at Zhejiang University, Hangzhou
Music Emotion: Development of iPad Application, eMello

May 2018 - August 2018

In a team of five members, we developed an eBook reader targeted for children. By employing Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning, eMello scans the contents of each page and plays background music to suit the current mood of the page.

My role was to design the User Interface of the application using Adobe XD, followed by XCode and Swift.

  • Adobe XD
  • XCode
  • Swift
  • Adobe Premiere Pro

Undergraduate Research Opportunities Programme (UROP)
Design and Construction of Novel String Musical Instrument

Aug 2017 - May 2018
